Premium apparel & headwear engineered for relentless hustle.

Motion was created on February 22, 2025 not just as a clothing brand, but as a statement. A brand fueled by relentless hustle and the discipline to turn dreams into reality.

It was built in a season when everything felt heavy. Doubts were loud. Challenges were real. Quitting would have been easy. But weakness was never an option. Even when life tried to slow things down, the mindset never changed:

"Stay on motion."

Motion represents more than apparel. It stands for constant forward movement pushing through pressure, grinding with purpose, and chasing success on your own terms. It's for those who refuse to sit on ideas and instead take bold action toward the life they want.

Every piece carries the story of ambition, resilience, and the pursuit of freedom through hard work. It reflects silent strength showing up daily even when no one sees the battles you're fighting. It's about keeping your standards high when circumstances are low.

TERMS OF SERVICE

Last updated: June 2025

1. ORDERS & PAYMENTS

All orders placed on MOTION Streetwear are subject to availability and payment confirmation. Prices are listed in Ghana Cedis (GHS). We accept MoMo, Visa, and Mastercard. Your order is only confirmed once payment has been successfully processed.

2. DELIVERY

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days. Delivery timelines vary by location. MOTION is not liable for delays caused by third-party courier services. You will be contacted via WhatsApp once your order is dispatched.

3. RETURNS & EXCHANGES

We accept exchanges within 7 days of delivery for unworn, unwashed items in their original condition. We do not offer refunds — only size exchanges or store credit. Items marked as final sale are not eligible for exchange. To initiate an exchange, contact us on WhatsApp.

4. CUSTOMER DATA & PRIVACY

Any information you provide during checkout is used solely to process and deliver your order. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Payment details are handled securely through our payment partners and are never stored on our servers.
